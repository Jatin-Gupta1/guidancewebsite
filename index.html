Timeline IIT (BHU) Varanasi

Banaras Hindu University was established in 1916 by Bharat Ratna Mahamana Pandit Madan Mohan Malviya ji.Prof.Charles KIng was appointed the first Principal and christened the college as "Benares Engineering College(BENCO)".Combined degree in Electrical and Mechanical engineering" was started.

Read more 1916-1919

The Depatment of Geology was started under BENCO in 1920.IN 1921 Department of Industrial Chemistry was established.In 1923 courses in Mining and Metallurgy were introduced.

Read more 1920s

In 1930,a chimney was established behind department of Mechanical Engineering.Mahamana's vision laid to the laying of the foundation stone of the campus'Vishwanath Temple'.In 1939 witnessed the formation of college of technology.

Read more 1930s

The main entrance gate and boundary walls of University was built.On 12 Nov,1946,the nation mourned the sad demise of Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya.1944 witnessed the formation of a separate College of Mining andMetallurgy(MINMET).

Read more 1940s

In 1950s the existing Industrial Chemistry department was reconstituted into a new Department of Chemical Engineering and Technology.The 1950s also saw the establishment of department of civil and Municipal Engineering.

Read more 1950s

Inthe years 1968-1971,the three colleges(BENCO,MINMETand TECHNO)were merged into one and the Institute of Technology was established.

Read more 1960s

In 1971,the department of Electronics Engineering was established.In 1972 regional admissions were replaced by admissions through the IIT-JEE.

Read more 1970s

Student Health Care Complex was established.Separate building for Institute's library was establishedThis was followed by establishment of Department of material science and technology,computer science and engineering.

Read more 1980s

The establishment of Department of Physics,Mathematical Sciences and Chemistry.the first editions of Kashiyatra and Spardha were held.Biomedical and Biochemical Engineering were also started.

Read more 1990s

Installation of Internet services.Gandhi Technology Alumni Centre was established.ON 29 june 2012,Institute of technology was renamed as Indian Institute of technology (BHU) Varanasi.Lecture theatres were also established .Also various new hostels were eastablished and first edition of FMC Weekend was witnessed.
